Our Craft

Since 2010, we've been dedicated to the art of coffee — from bean to cup.

100% Ethically Sourced

We partner directly with high-altitude farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Costa Rica to bring you exceptional microlot beans. Our head roaster develops each profile to highlight natural sweetness and complexity.

More than coffee, we've created a space where conversation flows, ideas bloom, and comfort is served daily. Come for the brew, stay for the community.
